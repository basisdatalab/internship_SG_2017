Profile of Telkom University

Telkom University (abbreviated as Tel-U) - is an amalgamation of several institutions under Telkom Education Foundation (YPT) which consist of IT Telkom, IM Telkom, Polytechnic Telkom and STISI Telkom. Tel-U specialized program of study in the field of "Information and Communications Technologies, Management and Creative Industries" in response to demands of rapidly ICT industry development.

The Mission

Organize and develop international standard education (1)
Advancing and disseminating science, technology (2)
Utilizing science, technology, management, and art (3)

Goals

(1) Achieving the trust of all stakeholders
(2) Creating an integrity and competence graduates with national and international competitiveness
(3) Constructing a research culture
(4) Producing beneficial research and innovation product
